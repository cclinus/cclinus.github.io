Openings


I plan to take one new PhD student (two if they are equally good) every year. Reach me out only if you are really interested in doing research. To join my research group, you should have a bachelor's/master's degree in computer science/engineering or mathematics, and have performed well academically. Further, you should have the following skills:

  • Good command over the English language (both spoken and written),

  • Excellent programming skills, and experience with developing systems software (e.g., operating systems kernels, compilers, middleware),

  • Research experience in the form of internships, research projects and/or papers in international venues,

  • Ability to work independently and be self-driven
